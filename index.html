Rooted here.
Worn anywhere.

Rooted Creations Co. is a one-person studio born out of a love for hand-drawn type, local pride, and the feeling of putting on a shirt that actually means something. Every design starts as a sketch, every print runs through our hands.

We work with neighbors, friends, and small businesses across San Antonio — and ship to people across Texas who carry a piece of SA wherever they go. No template work. No mass production. Just original goods, made to leave a mark.
